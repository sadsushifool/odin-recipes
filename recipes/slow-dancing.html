Description

Pluto Projector is a beautiful song that is meant to tell the story of a man who is unsure of whether or not he can add up to being all his lover wants him to be.

Instruments

  • Soft yet deep Piano
  • Stunning, Whispy Vocals
  • Epic and impactful Drums
  • A Sound That Engulfs You
  • Feelings Invested
  • A Sound Of many Emotions
  • Harmonized Vocals
  • An Epic Bridge
  • A constant build up with an epic close

Artist Info - Harry Styles

  1. 28 Years Old
  2. Birthday - February 4th, 1994
  3. Origin of Birth - Redditch, England
  4. First song made and released in 2010
  5. Formerly part of a band known as One Direction
  6. Inspirations - Pink Floyd, The Rolling Stones, The Beatles and Fleetwood Mac
