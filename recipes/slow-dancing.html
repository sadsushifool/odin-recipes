Description

Slow Dancing in the Dark is a song about heartbreak in the form of betrayal and self sabotage. This song paints a picture of how hard love can be and how your mentality can effect the other person.

Instruments

  • Impactful synths
  • An arpeggio of synths and harps
  • Epic and impactful Drums
  • A Sound That Engulfs You
  • Feelings Invested
  • A Sound Of many Emotions
  • Harmonized Vocals
  • Dark Vocals
  • Amazing Vocals
  • I just can't get over the vocals
  • Ends in a goosebumps like bliss
  • A constant build up with an epic close
